Our Story

The Movement (Tafiyar Matasa), also known simply as The Movement, was founded in August 2024 by a coalition of northern Nigerian activists and social media influencers, including Dr. Bulama Bukarti as its Chief Convener. It emerged as a powerful response to the frustrations of Nigerian youth, particularly after the #EndBadGovernance protest on August 1, 2024.

From its inception, The Movement has been dedicated to mobilizing young Nigerians to demand better governance and create positive change within their communities. Though it is still in the early stages of establishing its presence across all local government areas, The Movement has already begun setting up structures in several states, with plans to expand nationwide.

Our Mission

To empower Nigerian youth with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need to become active contributors to national development and agents of change in their communities.

Our Vision

Is to build a united, prosperous, and peaceful Nigeria where every young person can realize their full potential.
